## Notes

1. **Personalisation matters.** Generic "we'd love your input" emails are
   ignored. Cite a specific paper or talk by the recipient.
2. **Two-week silence ≠ rejection.** Senior researchers travel and
   review. Plan for 2-3 follow-ups before assuming no.
3. **Confidentiality.** Do not forward `PI_candidates_open_source_research.md`
   to recipients; it is internal planning material only.
4. **Attribution.** If a contact provides material help, offer
   co-authorship before submission revision.

# Advisory Committee — Recruitment Email Template

**Status:** Draft v0.1. Personalise per recipient before sending.
**Subject (en):** `OS-PM Advisory Committee — invitation to serve`
**Subject (ko):** `OS-PM 자문위원회 위원 초청 — [성명] 박사님께`

---

## English version (180-220 words)

```
Dear [Title] [Name],

I am [your name, position, affiliation], writing to invite you to
serve on the Advisory Committee of OS-PM, an open-source academic
reconstruction of the IAEA Physical Model. The project — released as
an arXiv preprint at [arXiv:TBD] and as code at github.com/[org]/os-pm
— provides a vision-language-model-driven, reproducible system for
academic safeguards research, with permanent exclusion of Volume 11
(weaponization) by schema-level policy.

We are forming a five-seat committee (Chair, two Technical seats
covering AI/VLM and GIS/RS, Policy, Ethics) for a renewable two-year
term. The role is uncompensated, with quarterly meetings (most
remote) and ad-hoc consultation on schema, case selection, and
release approvals. Co-authorship is offered for substantive
contributions per ICMJE criteria.

Your work on [specific paper / programme — to personalise] is one of
the foundational references the project builds on, and your
perspective would substantially strengthen our oversight. If you
are open to discussing further, I would be happy to share the full
charter and a 30-minute briefing at your convenience.

I attach the v1 preprint (8 pages), a 7-slide deck, and the draft
charter for your review.

Thank you for considering.

Sincerely,
[Your name]
[Affiliation], [Email] · ORCID: [orcid]
```

## Pre-send checklist

- [ ] 수신자 성명·소속·직위 확인 (publication record cross-check)
- [ ] "특정 논문/프로그램" 부분에 수신자의 실제 작업 1-2건 인용
- [ ] 브리핑 후보일 2개 제시 (선택)
- [ ] 첨부파일 3개 (preprint PDF + 데모 deck PDF + charter PDF)
- [ ] BCC 옵션 (소속기관 관리자 가시화) 결정
- [ ] 한국어 / 영어 / bilingual 결정 — 수신자 선호 반영

## Follow-up cadence

- 7일 후: 응답 없으면 한 차례 친절한 follow-up
- 14일 후: 응답 없으면 다른 채널 시도 (학회 / 동료 referral)
- 응답 후: 24시간 내 30분 미팅 일정 회신
- 미팅 후 24시간 내: thank-you note + 다음 단계 명시

# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant 2.1, summarised below.

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level
of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualised language or imagery, and unwelcome attention
- Trolling, insulting comments, personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Project-Specific Additions

OS-PM operates in a sensitive technical and policy domain. Contributors
agree to:

- Treat IAEA, member-state, and academic colleagues with professional
  respect.
- Avoid speculation about active proliferation cases — restrict
  discussion to publicly disclosed historical cases.
- Decline to assist anyone seeking to use the project for purposes
  inconsistent with its scope policy.

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be sent to the maintainers
(see `README.md`). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.

The full Contributor Covenant 2.1 text is at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
